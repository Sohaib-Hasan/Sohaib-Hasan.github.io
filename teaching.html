Teaching

Nine years in the classroom.

Undergraduate mathematics, taught with LaTeX-typeset notes and Manim-animated lectures — now paired with leading the department.

I've taught undergraduate mathematics at Horizon Degree & Commerce College since March 2017. My approach leans on the same tools I use in my own research and in PlotLab: LaTeX for precise, well-typeset course materials, and Manim-animated visuals to make abstract ideas — from measure theory to number theory — easier to actually see.

Since June 2024, I've also headed the Mathematics Department, which shifted part of my role from the classroom to department-wide academic operations.
